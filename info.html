How to Use the Chatbot

Getting Started

To interact with the classroom chatbot, follow these simple steps:

1. Open the Chatbot

Click on the "Open Chat" button on the main page. This will display the chatbot interface.

2. Ask Your Questions

Type your questions in the chat input area. Our chatbot is here to assist you with a variety of academic inquiries.

3. Explore Options

You can ask about various topics, including class schedules, assignment due dates, and you can also contact your Class Representative for further assistance.

4. Close the Chat

When you're done, click the "Close Chat" button to hide the chatbot interface.
